Remote Work Policy

1. Eligibility
Remote work is available to full-time employees who have completed at least six months of employment and whose roles are suitable for remote execution. Eligibility is determined by the employee's manager in consultation with HR.

2. Work Hours
Remote employees are expected to maintain their standard working hours and remain accessible via email, chat, and video conferencing during core hours of 10 AM to 4 PM in their local time zone.

3. Equipment and Security
The organization will provide a laptop and necessary software licenses. Employees must use a secure, password-protected Wi-Fi connection. Use of public Wi-Fi is prohibited unless a VPN is active.

4. Performance Expectations
Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees. Managers will conduct monthly check-ins to review goals and address any concerns.

5. Leave and Attendance
Remote employees must follow the same leave request procedures as on-site staff. Attendance at quarterly in-person team meetings is mandatory unless an exception is approved by HR.

6. Data Security
Employees must store all work files on approved cloud platforms such as the internal drive. Storing sensitive files on personal devices or unauthorized cloud services is strictly prohibited.

7. Expense Reimbursement
The company will reimburse up to 50 dollars per month for internet expenses upon submission of a valid invoice. Home office equipment beyond the standard laptop is not reimbursable.

8. Policy Violations
Failure to comply with this policy may result in revocation of remote work privileges and additional disciplinary action.
