Employee Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave
Full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave must be applied for at least 5 working days in advance through the HR portal. Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.

2. Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.

3. Maternity and Paternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per applicable law. Male employees are entitled to 15 days of paid paternity leave, to be availed within 6 months of the child's birth.

4. Casual Leave
Employees are entitled to 6 days of casual leave per year for personal matters. Casual leave cannot be combined with annual or sick leave. It cannot be carried forward.

5. Public Holidays
Employees are entitled to all gazetted public holidays as declared by the government. A list of public holidays is published by HR at the start of each year.

6. Leave Without Pay
Employees may apply for leave without pay after exhausting all eligible leave. Approval is at the discretion of the department head and HR. Extended leave without pay beyond 30 days requires written approval from the CEO.

7. Bereavement Leave
Employees are entitled to 3 days of paid bereavement leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member, defined as spouse, parent, child, or sibling.

8. Leave Encashment
Annual leave can be encashed at the time of resignation or retirement, up to a maximum of 30 days, subject to applicable tax deductions.
